Egg Omelette Recipe

Ingredients Needed:

2-3 Eggs
1/2 Tablespoon Butter
Salt (just a pinch)
Any small amount of meats, vegetables, or cheese

Recipe Steps:

1. First, prepare all of your ingredients and make them ready to use. Also, take out a small size nonstick pan.
2. Second, in a small bowl, combine 2-3 eggs and put in a pinch of salt. Then, mix it with a fork until it's frothy.
3. Third, place the pan over medium heat and place your 1/2 Tbsp butter. Until melted, add the eggs and reduce to low heat.
4. Fourth, when the eggs are nearly set, you can get a saptula to put under the eggs and flip the eggs over.
5. Fifth, you can turn off the heat. Now add your favorite toppings (2-5 toppings recommended). Fold the omelette and serve on the plate!
6. Enjoy the delicious breakfast egg omelette!

Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients Needed:

French or Italian Loaf Bread
Mayonnaise
Mustard (Optional)
American Cheese (Slices)
Oven Roasted Turkey (Slices)
Lettuce
Tomato
Red Onion
Banana Peppers
Red Wine Vinegar (Optional)
Olive Oil (Optional)
Salt
Black Pepper
Italian Seasoning
Garlic Powder

Recipe Steps:

1. First, make your vegetables ready to slice and chop.
2. Second, chop the tomatoes and onions into slice pieces, and chop the lettuce into small pieces like a salad.
3. Third, slice your french or italian bread in half, but not all the way through. Make sure your bread is able to open up in half.
4. Fourth, spread mayonnaise on both sides of the bread.
5. Fifth, sprinkle all of the seasonings from the ingredients list onto the mayonnaise. Sprinkle according to your taste.
6. Sixth, put turkey slices (slices depend on size of bread) onto the bread. Then, put American cheese on top of the turkey slices.
7. Seventh, add your tomato slices onto the sandwich. Then, add slices of onions. Then add your banana peppers. Add chopped lettuce to the other side of the bread.
8. Eighth, add a mixture of olive oil and red wine vinegar as your flavorful condiment, or you can add mustard.
9. Finally, on this last step, fold your sandwich, and cut it according to what you'll eat. Enjoy the delicious lunch turkey sandwich!

Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients Needed:

Spaghetti (1 Package 500G)
Ground Beef (1 lb)
Water (600ml)
Onions (1, chopped)
Red Bell Pepper (1, chopped)
Garlic Cloves (3, minced)
Tomato Sauce (22oz)
Tomato Paste (2 Tbsp)
Olive Oil (2-3 Tbsp)
Salt (1 Tbsp)
Black Pepper (1/2 Tbsp)
Dried Oregano (1 Tbsp)
Parsley (Handful chopped)

Recipe Steps:

1. First, take out a large pan. Put the olive oil onto the pan. Turn it to high heat.
2. Second, once your pan is hot, add your ground beef. Cook it and brown the beef on high heat.
3. Third, when the beef is brown, add your chopped onions, red bell pepper, and minced garlic onto the beef. Cook until they are soft.
4. Fourth, turn it down to medium heat, and add the tomato paste. Cook it for 2 minutes stirring it frequently on the pan. Then, add your tomato sauce.
5. Fifth, using the tomato sauce can, fill the can with a quarter of the way with water and pour onto the pan.
6. Sixth, let the pan to boil and reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, until it thickens to your own liking.
7. Seventh, season the sauce with the meat with salt, black pepper, and dried oregano. When the sauce is ready, chop the parsley and add it onto it.
8. Eighth, stir your bolognese sauce and turn the heat off.
9. Ninth, cook your spaghetti according to the box/package directions. When done, serve on a plate.
10. Tenth, pour your bolognese sauce over the spaghetti on your plate. Get a fork and enjoy the delicious spaghetti bolognese!
